Priority Action Plan

Ranked by impact. Each item includes data justification.

🔴 Priority 1 — Fix Tracking & Clean Data (This Week)

1. Set up GA4 key events. Mark form_submit (50 occurrences), video_complete (58), and contact page visits (189 views) as key events. Why: Zero conversions tracked = zero ROI visibility.

2. Create bot traffic filter. Exclude China + Singapore traffic in GA4. GSC confirms 0-1 clicks from these countries vs 8,579 GA4 users. Why: 76% of your data is garbage.

3. Fix duplicate URLs. /time-and-materials-vs-fixed-fee (no slash) has 48 GA4 views splitting from the main page. /articles and /articles/ are split too. Add 301 redirects. Why: Your #1 GSC page has 42,689 impressions — don't split that authority.

🟡 Priority 2 — Fix CTR Crisis (This Month)

4. Rewrite title tags & meta descriptions for top GSC pages. Your T&M page has 42,689 impressions at 0.12% CTR. Even improving to 2% CTR = 854 clicks (vs current 52). That's a 16x increase from one page. Target pages: /time-and-materials-vs-fixed-fee/, /6-components-of-a-change-management-plan/, /when-is-overtime-ok/, /achieve-100-productivity/.

5. Add FAQ schema to T&M and change management pages. "t&m meaning" has 1,471 impressions at position 2.7 with 0 clicks. A featured snippet or FAQ rich result could capture hundreds of monthly clicks. Why: Position 1-3 queries with 0 clicks = broken snippets.

6. Redesign homepage CTAs. 4,667 views at 5.6s engagement. Add pathways to /experience/, /green-dot-system/, /consult/. Why: 36% of traffic lands here and bounces.

🟢 Priority 3 — Scale What Works (Next Quarter)

7. Create dedicated change management content. "Change management plan" has 1,601 impressions but you rank at position 69. A comprehensive guide could capture this traffic. Why: ~20K impressions in this topic cluster with nearly 0 clicks.

8. Expand T&M content hub. Create sub-pages for "t&m meaning" (1,471 imp), "t&m vs fixed price" (2,012 imp), "time and material pricing" (687 imp). Why: Your T&M page already has massive visibility — own the entire topic.

9. Improve email campaigns. 365 sessions at 44% engagement. Segment lists and link to /experience/ and /learn/academy/. Why: 2nd most reliable channel.

10. Build referral partnerships. 59.6% engagement from referrals. Why: Nearly as good as organic with less effort.

💡 The Bottom Line

Envisor has 83,258 search impressions and only 661 clicks. That's like having a billboard on a highway and covering it with a tarp. Your content is ranking, Google is showing it — but searchers aren't clicking. The single highest-ROI action is rewriting title tags and meta descriptions for your top 10 GSC pages. Conservatively, improving CTR from 0.79% to 2% would take you from 661 to ~1,665 organic clicks per 6 months — a 152% increase with zero new content. Combined with bot filtering and conversion tracking, you'll finally see the real picture and can make data-driven decisions.
